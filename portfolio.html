Animal Abuse Poster Series

 This is one poster of a three piece series for animal abuse. The poster is designed to bring recognition to animal abuse and calls the audience to help by redirecting them to the ASPCA website. The three series posters are of dogs, cats and horses, the three most statistically abused animals.

Tigerlight Logo Redesign

 This piece is a redesign of a company called Tigerlight. This new concept i designed was designed for a simplicity with impact making the tiger detailed but still visible at a very small size. I went with a san serif font because it is a very bold and masculine product.

Typographic Anatomy Poster

 This poster was designed with influence from Robert Bringhurts "The Elements of Typographic Style" and Ellen Luptin in "thinking with Type". It is an informative poster on the anatomy of typography as well as elemental breakdowns of type.
